Dallas joined the Wild Rose collective after seven years of coaching and
mentorship with founder Paige Smith. In 2026 he makes the jump to the
Disc Golf Pro Tour — the first Wild Rose athlete to do so.

Dallas carries two Wild Rose signature discs on tour: the Light-Speeded
and the Stokely. Both were designed and sampled alongside Paige, and
every profile sale on this page sends a cut directly back to Dallas.
